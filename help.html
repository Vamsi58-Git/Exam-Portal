Help & Guidelines

How SGPA & CGPA are calculated (demo)

This static demo uses a simplified calculation for demonstration.

  1. Each subject has a marks value (for demo we show marks out of 100).
  2. SGPA is calculated as (Total Marks / (Number of subjects * 100)) * 10 — scaled to 10.
  3. CGPA is a multi-semester average of SGPAs (in real systems you keep cumulative credits into account).

Test Instructions

  • Make sure you are registered and logged in as a student before starting the test.
  • Once you click Start Test, the 30 minute timer begins.
  • Questions appear one-by-one. Use Next/Previous to navigate. Use the Finish or Submit button when done.
  • Do not refresh the page during the test. This demo stores answers locally; in production you'd save periodically to server.
  • At the end, results are saved and viewable in the Results page by entering your roll number.

Privacy Policy (short)

This is a static demo. All user data (registration & test attempts) is stored locally on your browser (localStorage). In a real deployment:

  • We would store hashed passwords and encrypted records on secure servers.
  • Only authorized admins can view student results.
  • Personal data will not be shared with third parties without consent.
